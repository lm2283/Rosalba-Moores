About

Rosalba graduated from the College of Naturopathic Medicine and is a member of the BANT organisation and the Association of Naturopathic Practitioners. She is also CNHC registered. Rosalba currently practices at the London Wellbeing & Nutrition Clinic, where she aims to empower people to take control of their health, and to make sustainable, achievable and long-term health changes.
